My View我的观点02 / 06

Raising capital for crypto isn't about hype — it's about conviction backed by homework. The allocators who matter want someone who understands the asset as deeply as they do. 为加密基金募资,靠的不是炒作 —— 而是有功课支撑的信念。真正重要的配置者,想要的是一个对资产理解得和他们一样深的人。

01

Crypto trades as a risk assetCrypto 是风险资产

Crypto is increasingly correlated with the Nasdaq — it now behaves as a high-beta risk asset, not an isolated story. The implication is simple: allocate it to your risk appetite, sized like the risk asset it has become.Crypto 正与纳斯达克走向高相关,越来越像一个高 beta 的风险资产,而非独立叙事。含义很清楚:按自己的风险偏好来配置它,用对待风险资产的方式定仓位。

02

From narrative to cash flow从叙事到现金流

Crypto's next chapter is institutional maturation — moving from narrative-driven hype toward assets with real, durable cash flow, much like the early internet did.Crypto 的下一章是机构成熟化 —— 从叙事驱动的炒作,走向拥有真实、稳定现金流的方向,就像早期互联网走过的路。

03

TradFi meets Web3 in Asia亚洲:传统金融与 Web3 融合

As Singapore and Hong Kong open up and support crypto, the trend is clear: traditional finance and Web3 digital finance converging — institution-led.随着新加坡与香港政策逐步开放、支持 crypto,趋势已清晰:传统金融与 Web3 数字金融走向融合 —— 由机构主导。
